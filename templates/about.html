About Us

Built to make digital communication feel thoughtful, interactive, and completely secure.

🛡️

Zero Data Storage. Total Privacy.

Most e-card websites force you to create an account so they can harvest your email and track your data. We do the exact opposite.

Your card lives completely inside the link you generate. We use advanced compression to pack all your custom colors, text, and pages directly into the URL itself. Because of this, your card data is never saved, stored, or processed on our servers. What you write is strictly between you and the recipient.

💖

Powered by Voluntary Support

We hate feature-gating. You shouldn't have to pay $5 just to add an extra page to a birthday card. Every feature on this site is 100% free.

To keep the servers running and the site ad-free for those who want it, we rely entirely on voluntary support. If you love the tool, you can buy us a coffee via donation, or simply click the supporter link to generate revenue!
