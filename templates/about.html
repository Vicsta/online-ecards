About the Project

Bridging the gap between the physical warmth of a traditional greeting card and the limitless potential of modern web technology.

💡

Our Mission

Digital communication has become fast but impersonal. We wanted to build a platform that brings back the intentionality of a physical card—the act of opening, turning pages, and discovering a message—all powered by modern 3D web rendering.

⚙️

The Technology

Built using lightweight vanilla JavaScript and CSS 3D transforms, the engine renders interactive elements entirely in your browser. By utilizing URL-based data compression, your messages stay private because they aren't stored on a central server.
